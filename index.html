About

Okay, what?

We're Jebbit. We are the industry leaders in the revolutionary Post-Click Engagement™ technology, we work in the best office in the city, we have the best employees in the world, and we party better than any other startup in Boston.

But what even is Jebbit Labs?

Jebbit Labs is a place for us to showcase our work beyond the platform. Jebbit Labs is meant to be a collection of tools, utilities, and fun little apps for not only us to use, but for the community as well. Everything's open source! Click on a project, and check out the GitHub page. We ♥ pull requests!
